Questions

Things people usually ask

Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. I handle everything on the technical side. You just tell me how your business works and what's eating up your time. I build systems that are simple for you and your team to use.
What does the Shadow Day involve?
I spend a full working day at your business, observing your team's workflows, asking questions, and mapping out every process that could be automated. At the end, you get a detailed blueprint document that lays out exactly what to automate, how it would work, and the time and cost savings you'd see.
How much does it cost?
The audit call is completely free. The Shadow Day and implementation depend on the size and complexity of your business — I'll give you a clear quote after the audit once I understand your situation. No surprises. Most clients see a return on their investment within the first month.
How long until I see results?
Most automations go live within 2-4 weeks of starting the build. Some quick wins from the audit can be implemented even sooner. You start saving time from day one.
What if I already use software like Xero, HubSpot, or Google Workspace?
Great, that makes things easier. The automations I build connect to the tools you already use. I'm not replacing your existing systems, I'm making them work together so things happen automatically instead of manually.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes. After the build, I offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing optimization, monitoring, and building new automations as your business evolves. But there's no lock-in. Everything I build, you own.
